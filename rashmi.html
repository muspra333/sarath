Rashmika Mandann

( born 5 April 1996)[1] is an Indian actress who works
predominantly in Telugu and Kannada films in addition to Tamil and Hindi films.[2] She is a recipient
of four SIIMA Awards and a Filmfare Award South. Her most commercially successful films include Kirik Party (2016), Anjani
Putra (2017), Yajamana (2019), Sarileru Neekevvaru (2020), Bheeshma (2020), Pogaru (2021), Pushpa: The Rise (2021), Sita Ramam (2022)
and Varisu (2023). She won the Filmfare Critics Award for Best Actress – South for her performance in
the Telugu romantic comedy Geetha Govindam (2018).
